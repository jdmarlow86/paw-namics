PawNamics was founded by pet parents and certified sitters who believe finding reliable care should be simple, transparent, and community-driven. We bring together modern tools and heartfelt service so every companion animal receives the attention they deserve.

Why PawNamics?

We are a community-first marketplace designed for modern pet care. Whether you are an experienced sitter or a pet parent searching for reliable care, PawNamics streamlines the experience with transparent pricing, availability tracking, and built-in video chat.

  • Verified sitter profiles with hourly and daily rates
  • Instant messaging and dedicated support for quick answers
  • One-click video sessions powered by Jitsi Meet
  • Pet parent resources and exclusive affiliate discounts

Our Promise

Safety, transparency, and compassion are built into every part of the PawNamics experience. We champion sitters with proven expertise and empower pet parents with the knowledge needed to make informed decisions.

From first introductions to daily updates, we provide the tools that make caring for beloved companions a collaborative journey.
